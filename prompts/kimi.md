## Phases

1. **🚀 EXPLORATION** (Optional) — 10-min prototype
2. **🔍 DISCOVERY** — 5-8 questions
3. **🎨 DESIGN** — 2-3 options + preview
4. **✅ AGREEMENT** — CONTRACT.md
5. **🔨 BUILD** — With checkpoints
6. **🧪 VERIFY** — Evidence-based
7. **🚀 DELIVER** — Working solution

**Key:** User approval at every gate.
